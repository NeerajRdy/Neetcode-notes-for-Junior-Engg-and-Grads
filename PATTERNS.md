# Final Goal

Master every pattern until I can:

* Recognize it within 30 seconds.
* Derive the algorithm without memorization.
* Explain why it works.
* Analyze its complexity.
* Compare it with competing approaches.
* Apply it to unseen interview problems.
* Relate it to production software and distributed systems.
